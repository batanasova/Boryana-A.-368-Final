Boryana A Atanasova, is a Bulgarian Graphic Designer. Raised in Bulgaria, her family made the tough decision to move from Eastern Europe to the United States of America. As she progressed through her education, she became involved in many of the after school sports programs such as: Volleyball, Javelin, Discus, etc. Building herself up to succeed, and gain a level of fame within the sports community. As well as building onto her love of the arts. After overcoming language barriers and many other challenges, she graduated from Renton High School, Then Bellevue College. Then she began down her route to a fine-arts degree in graphic design. Starting with photography and working her way through digital and traditional mediums. Ultimately drop kicking her way into the web-development world with a vengeance. As she ventures into more, she will continue to update and add more creativity into the world. Remain humbled and never forgetting her background and love for art.
